Eve the Eavesdropper

In this video game, you play as Eve—a brilliant cryptographer once trusted with safeguarding federal secrets. Tasked with securing sensitive communications, she uncovered a darker reality: criminal syndicates were masking their operations behind ciphers and encoded messages. But when she sounded the alarm, the government chose silence. Outraged and unwilling to let corruption flourish, Eve vanished from her post and reemerged as a rogue vigilante. Armed with nothing but her intellect and a custom toolkit, she now intercepts underground transmissions, decodes their secrets, and rewrites their contents to pit crime bosses against one another. She doesn’t just break codes—she breaks empires.
